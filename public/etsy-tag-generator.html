Why your Etsy tags decide whether you get found

Etsy's search shows buyers the listings whose title and tags best match what they typed. If your 13 tags don't cover the phrases real shoppers use, your product simply doesn't appear — no matter how good the photos are. Tags are the cheapest, fastest lever you have on Etsy SEO.

The 3 rules of high-ranking Etsy tags

  1. Use all 13. Every empty tag is a search you'll never show up for. Fill every slot with a distinct phrase.
  2. Go multi-word. The 20-character limit is enough for phrases like "lavender soy candle" — these "long-tail" phrases have less competition and higher buyer intent than single words like "candle".
  3. Match buyer language, not product jargon. Shoppers search "gift for new mom", not "postpartum present". Mirror the words a customer would type.
