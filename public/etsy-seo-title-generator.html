Why the first words of your title matter most

Etsy reads your title left to right and weighs the opening words most heavily — and so do shoppers skimming results. A title that starts with "Handmade Gift…" wastes that prime space. One that starts with "Lavender Soy Candle…" tells Etsy exactly what you sell on the very first match.

Anatomy of a high-ranking Etsy title

  • Front-load the primary keyword: the exact phrase a buyer would type goes first.
  • Layer 2–3 supporting keywords: related phrases a shopper might also search, separated by commas or bars.
  • Stay readable: a wall of keywords reads as spam and can hurt conversion. It should still make sense to a human.
  • Use the space: up to 140 characters — but never pad with filler words that no one searches.
