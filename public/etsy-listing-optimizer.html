What "optimized" actually means on Etsy

Etsy ranks listings on relevance, listing quality, and recency. You control relevance most directly — through the words in your title, tags, and description. An optimized listing isn't keyword-stuffed; it puts the phrases buyers search where Etsy weighs them most.

The 5 parts of a high-converting listing

  • Title: lead with your single strongest search phrase, then layer in supporting keywords — front-loaded, not buried.
  • 13 tags: every slot filled with a distinct long-tail phrase under 20 characters.
  • Description: first two lines repeat your main keywords and state the benefit — this is what shows in Google and the preview.
  • Materials & attributes: add legitimate search surface and help Etsy categorize you.
  • Image alt text: accessibility plus a small SEO signal most sellers skip.
